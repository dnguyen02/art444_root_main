FAQs

What career opportunities are available after graduating from SDSU?

Graduates from SDSU can explore a wide range of career paths, including business, education, healthcare, engineering, and the arts.

Are there any graduate programs or further education options?

Yes, many graduates pursue master's degrees in business administration (MBA), public health, education, and other specialized fields.

How can I leverage my SDSU network to find job opportunities?

Graduates can use platforms like LinkedIn and alumni associations to connect and network.

What resources does SDSU provide for career transition?

SDSU offers career counseling, resume workshops, job fairs, and mentorship programs.
